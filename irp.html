Roller Coasters

History-

  • The first roller coasters were believed to have been based off of the Russian ice slides constructed for Catherine the Great outside St. Petersburg
  • The first roller coaster (Gravity Switchback Railway) was made by LaMarcus Thompson in 1884 in Coney Island, Brooklyn, New York.
  • Though LaMarcus Adna Thompson obtained a patent regarding roller coasters he obtained the idea from several other sources
  • The Gravity Switchback Railway made 600$ a day from eager riders
  • 1885 Philip Hinkle invented first complete circuit with a lift hill
  • 1912 first underfriction roller coaster
  • 1929-1972 roller coaster were ignored because of the Great Depression, WWI, and WWII
  • Disneyland invented 1959 first steel roller coaster built
  • The Racer was built by John Allen in 1972 starts second golden age of roller coasters
  • First roller coaster to perform a corkscrew back in 1975 in Knotts Berry Farm , Buena Park California
